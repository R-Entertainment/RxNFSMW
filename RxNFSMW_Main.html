A little showcase of the Blacklist wreaking havoc in Rockport City from the
most successful Need for Speed title, Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2005).


The Blacklist is a list of racers as bosses of the game that are highly targeted for capture by the
Rockport Police Department due to their illegal street activities and damage to properties.
They're ranked based on the placed bounty on them. Defeating all of them
will make the player the MOST WANTED.

Click on a Blacklist's car to know more about them.
